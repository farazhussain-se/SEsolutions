# Ross Stores Inc. — Demo-Relevant Facts

Verified 2026-05-20 / 2026-05-23 from primary sources (Ross IR, BGCA newsroom, Ross corporate sustainability page). For any Ross demo work, **start here** to avoid attribution errors.

## Company essentials

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Legal name | Ross Stores Inc. |
| Ticker | NASDAQ: **ROST** |
| HQ | Dublin, California — internally called **"Home Office"**, never "HQ" |
| Brand portfolio | **Ross Dress for Less** (flagship) + **dd's DISCOUNTS** (subsidiary banner) |
| Model | Off-price retail — opportunistic buying, fast DC flow, no promo cadence, "treasure hunt" in-store experience |

## Footprint

- ~**1,800** Ross Dress for Less stores
- ~**350** dd's DISCOUNTS stores
- **8** distribution centers — known locations:
  - Carlisle, PA
  - Perris, CA
  - Fort Mill, SC
  - Rock Hill, SC
  - Moreno Valley, CA
- **Buying offices** in **New York City** and **Los Angeles** (not centralized — geographically split between East/West merchandising teams)
- 2025 store expansion completed: ~90 new locations across 17 states (80 Ross + 10 dd's), including new-market entries into **Puerto Rico** and the **New York Metro** area
- Long-term plan: **2,900** Ross + **700** dd's locations

## Philanthropy — Ross Stores Foundation

- 16+ year partnership with **Boys & Girls Clubs of America** (BGCA)
- **Over $50 million** contributed by Ross and customers to BGCA over the partnership
- Renewed for 3 more years — announced 2025-01-31
- Annual in-store **round-up fundraiser** Feb–Mar each year; Foundation matches up to **$500K**
- Source: https://www.bgca.org/news-stories/2025/January/ross-dress-for-less-and-boys-girls-clubs-of-america-extend-and-expand-longstanding-partnership-to-give-more-kids-great-futures/

## Sustainability (most recent CSR cycle)

- **31% reduction** in Scope 1+2 GHG emissions per square foot vs. **2017** baseline (exceeded the 30%-by-2025 target)
- Interim target: **42% absolute** Scope 1+2 reduction by **2030** (vs. 2021 baseline)
- Long-term: **net-zero** GHG emissions by **2050 or sooner**
- **66%** landfill waste diversion
- CDP 2024 Climate Change disclosure score: **B**
- Source: https://corp.rossstores.com/responsibility/operating-sustainably/

## Key terminology to use (and not use)

| Use | Don't use | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Home Office | HQ / Corporate HQ | Ross internal convention |
| Associates | Employees | Standard retail convention |
| Treasure hunt | Bargain hunt / Discount hunt | The official brand promise |
| Off-price | Discount / Outlet | Off-price is the specific industry term + Ross's model |
| Buying office | Buying HQ / Buying HQ NYC | Singular per location ("the NYC buying office") |
| Stores | Retail outlets / Branches | Stores |
